# Poppa P's Poker Night — Text Updates

Get a text before each poker night at **Poppa P's** cigar lounge, plus the
occasional promo, and climb the season standings. This page explains the SMS
program, what's tracked, how to join, and how to stop.

> **Program name:** Poppa P's Poker Night
> **Message from:** (615) 695-1691

---

## How to join (opt in)

Players opt in **in person at the lounge**, after they've been ID'd and bought
their cigars. To join, **text `JOIN` to (615) 695-1691** from your own phone.

We'll text back asking for your **first name and last initial** (e.g. "Mike R") —
reply with it and you're on the list. That name is how you appear on the
standings (see below).

By texting `JOIN`, you agree to receive recurring automated SMS from Poppa P's:
game reminders **and occasional promos**. **Consent is not a condition of any
purchase.**

## What you'll get

- A reminder text before each scheduled game (typically the day before).
- The occasional **promo** (e.g. a 2-for-1 at the bar for regulars) and
  tournament invite.
- Schedule changes or heads-ups.

**Message frequency varies** — usually only a few messages per month.

## What we track (and show publicly)

Once you're in, we track your **game results (points)** and **attendance** so we
can run the season standings, the quarterly Special Players tournament, and
attendance promos.

Your **first name and last initial** (e.g. "Mike R") and your point total appear
on a **public standings page**. We never show your full name or phone number
publicly. If you don't opt in, none of this is tracked.

## Cost

**Message and data rates may apply**, depending on your mobile plan. Poppa P's
doesn't charge anything for the texts.

## How to stop (opt out)

Reply **`STOP`** to any message at any time and you'll be removed immediately.
You'll get one final confirmation text and nothing after that. To rejoin later,
just text `JOIN` again.

## Need help?

Reply **`HELP`** to any message, or ask the host at Poppa P's.

## Your privacy

We store your mobile number, your display name (first name + last initial), and
your game results/attendance — used only to run reminders, promos, and the
standings. **We never sell or share your number.** See the
[Privacy Policy](./privacy.md) for details.

---

## About the game

Poppa P's poker night is a private, social game hosted at the cigar lounge.
Players purchase cigars up front — and are ID'd at purchase — and then compete
for cigars at the table. **No money is wagered and there are no cash payouts.**
The text program is for game reminders, promos, and standings for players who
ask to be included.
