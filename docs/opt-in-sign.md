#### The details

- You'll get **recurring** game reminders and occasional promos by text.
  **Message frequency varies** (usually a few a month).
- **Message & data rates may apply.**
- Once you join, your **first name + last initial** (e.g. "Mike R") and your
  points appear on a **public standings page**. We never show your full name or
  number.
- Reply **STOP** to cancel any time. Reply **HELP** for help.
- **Consent is not a condition of any purchase.**

---

#### The fine print

This is a private, social game at the lounge. Players buy cigars up front — and
are ID'd at purchase — and compete for cigars at the table. **No money is wagered
and there are no cash payouts.** The text program is just reminders, promos, and
standings for players who ask to be included.
