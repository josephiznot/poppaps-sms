# Privacy Policy — Poppa P's Poker Night SMS

_Last updated: 2026-06-08_

This policy covers the SMS program for Poppa P's poker night (reminders, promos,
and the season standings).

## What we collect

- Your **mobile phone number**.
- A **display name** you give us at opt-in — your **first name and last initial**
  (e.g. "Mike R").
- Your **opt-in / opt-out status** and timestamps.
- Your **game results (points)** and **attendance** at games.
- Basic message metadata (e.g. when we last texted you).

We do **not** collect your email, address, location, or any payment information
through this program.

## How we use it

- To text you **game reminders, promos, and tournament invites**.
- To track **points and attendance** so we can run season **standings**, the
  quarterly Special Players tournament, and attendance-based promos.
- To show your **first name + last initial and point total on a public standings
  page** (see below).

## Public standings

The standings page shows your **first name + last initial** (e.g. "Mike R") and
your points. We **never** display your full name, your phone number, or any other
personal detail publicly. Opting in is what puts you on the board; if you don't
opt in, you aren't tracked or shown.

## What we don't do

- We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your number or name.
- We do **not** share your information with third parties for their own marketing.

We use **Twilio** to deliver the texts on our behalf, and mobile carriers to
transmit them. They don't use your information beyond delivering these messages.

## Opting out

Reply **STOP** to any message to stop all texts immediately. We keep a record
that you opted out so we don't text you again. Your past standings/results may
remain in the season history; ask the host to remove them if you'd like.

## Data retention

We keep your information while you participate and for a reasonable period after,
so we can honor opt-outs and keep season history accurate. You can ask us to
delete your record entirely by contacting the host at Poppa P's.

## Contact

Questions about this program or your data? Ask the host at Poppa P's, or reply
**HELP** to any message.
