# Privacy Policy — Poppa P's Poker Night Text Reminders

_Last updated: 2026_

This policy covers the SMS reminder program for Poppa P's poker night.

## What we collect

- Your **mobile phone number**.
- Your **opt-in / opt-out status** and the timestamps of those actions.
- Basic message metadata (e.g. when we last texted you).

We do **not** collect your name, email, location, or any payment information
through this text program.

## How we use it

We use your number for one purpose only: to send you reminders and updates about
upcoming poker games at Poppa P's. That's it.

## What we don't do

- We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your number.
- We do **not** share it with third parties for their own marketing.
- We do **not** use it for any program other than these game reminders.

We do use **Twilio** as our messaging provider to deliver the texts on our
behalf. Mobile carriers transmit the messages. Neither uses your number for
anything beyond delivering these messages.

## Opting out

Reply **STOP** to any message to be removed immediately. We keep a record that
you opted out so we don't text you again, but we stop sending messages.

## Data retention

We keep your number while you're subscribed and for a reasonable period after
you opt out, so we can honor your opt-out. You can ask us to delete your record
entirely by contacting the host at Poppa P's.

## Contact

Questions about this program or your data? Ask the host at Poppa P's, or reply
**HELP** to any message.
